Who We Are

Practical Electronics, Built with Purpose

Cytron Technology Sdn. Bhd is a technology company dedicated to developing innovative solutions for industrial and automation applications to makers, educators, and professionals. We create technology that is accessible and impactful.

How We Build

From The Ground Up By Engineers, For Engineers.

With an in-house design and engineering team, we develop our own products from the ground up covering design, manufacturing, and community. Guided by real user needs, we build solutions from a user's perspective to ensure practicality, reliability, and ease of use.
